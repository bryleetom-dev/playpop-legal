About PlayPop

Version 1.0.0

Place pieces, create matches, trigger combos, and achieve the highest score possible across a variety of challenges.

PlayPop is a puzzle game focused on strategic decision-making, efficient board management, and progression through increasingly difficult levels.

Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose.

This application is free to play and may display advertisements, including optional rewarded advertisements, to support ongoing development.
